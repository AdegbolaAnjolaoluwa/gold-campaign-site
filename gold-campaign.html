The Reality We Face

THE PROBLEMS
IN COLAW

01
😔

Social Isolation & Burnout

Law students are buried under intense academic pressure with little to no organized social outlets, leading to burnout, anxiety, and disconnection from peers.

02
🏚️

Weak Class Community

There is a lack of bonding activities across levels. Students from different years rarely interact, creating a fragmented and divided student body in COLAW.

03
📅

Poor Event Planning & Execution

Past social events have been poorly organized, under-publicized, and poorly attended. Students feel events don't reflect their interests or celebrate their identity as law students.

04
🤝

Lack of Networking Opportunities

There are virtually no structured opportunities for law students to build professional networks, connect with alumni, or engage with legal professionals during their time on campus.

05
🎓

No Culture of Celebration

Academic milestones, birthdays, and student achievements go unrecognized. COLAW lacks a culture of celebrating its students and honouring their progress and excellence.

06
📢

Student Voice Not Heard

Students rarely have input into the social agenda. Events are planned without consulting the student body, leaving the majority unrepresented and disengaged.

Gold's Blueprint

MY SOLUTIONS
FOR COLAW

✓

Monthly COLAW Social Events

Structured, consistent monthly events: game nights, dinner socials, themed hangouts, designed to decompress law students and build real friendships across levels.

✓

Cross-Level Bonding Programs

Organized mixers and mentorship activities that connect 100-level students with finalists, building a strong, unified COLAW family and a lasting sense of belonging.

✓

Law Networking Nights

Termly events that bring in COLAW alumni, legal practitioners, and professionals to help students build career connections while still in school.

✓

Student-Led Event Planning

A transparent event committee where students submit ideas and vote on social activities, ensuring every event truly reflects what COLAW students want.

✓

Culture of Recognition & Celebration

A dedicated program to celebrate academic wins, birthdays, and milestones, making every COLAW student feel seen, valued, and proud of their community.
